Key Takeaways

1. Customers almost never call back (~0.9%). When an expert calls during an FS appointment and the customer doesn't answer, fewer than 1 in 100 will initiate a return call. This is even lower than the all-CG rate (3.6%) because FS customers expect the expert to manage the process.
2. The system works — 64% eventually reconnect. Even though customers rarely call back, 63.5% of affected customers eventually have a later FS call handled through expert/system-initiated re-engagement. The expert or scheduling system drives reconnection, not the customer.
3. Expert retries within 30 min connect 100% of the time. Of the 116 expert retries on the same engagement, those within 30 minutes had a perfect connection rate. Beyond 4 hours, the success rate drops to ~50–60%. Faster retries = better outcomes.
4. ~36% of customers have no reconnection yet. 336 customers (35.6%) who missed the call have not yet reconnected via any FS channel. Many are recent (Feb–Mar), so some will still reconnect. But this cohort represents potential at-risk engagements worth monitoring.
5. Product completion is too early to measure. With most missed calls in Feb–Mar 2026, FS returns are still being prepared by experts. Completion rates will mature as the April filing deadline approaches. Only 4 of 943 customers have completed any product so far.
Methodology & Data Source

Source table: ent_care_dwh.rpt_cct_interactions (CCT — Contact Trace Records)

Period: TYTD, Nov 2025 – Mar 2026 (leg_start_yearmonth >= 202511)

Scope: bu = 'cg', interaction_skill_function = 'FS', appointment_id IS NOT NULL

Expert missed call definition:
FS appointment calls where the customer did not answer:
interaction_skill_function = 'FS'
AND appointment_id IS NOT NULL
AND customer_handle_flg = 0
AND initiated_type NOT IN ('INBOUND VOICE') — excludes customer-initiated calls
Primary type: VOICE CALLBACK SCHEDULED (96% of FS appt missed calls)

Customer callback: initiated_type = 'INBOUND VOICE' on same engagement_id after the miss

Expert retry: Any subsequent expert-initiated call on same engagement (excluding INBOUND VOICE)

Later FS call handled: Any customer_handle_flg = 1 call with interaction_skill_function = 'FS' for same auth_id after the miss

Product completion: tax_rpt.product_analytics_master with tax_year = 2025, joined on auth_id. Note: 86% of affected customers have no PAM record yet — FS returns are prepared by experts before the customer reviews and files.

Previous version note: An earlier version of this report included ALL CG outbound calls (DIY support, TTL, etc.), not just FS appointment calls. This version correctly filters to interaction_skill_function = 'FS' + appointment_id IS NOT NULL, reducing the population from 36,040 to 1,050 and the callback rate from 3.6% to 0.9%.
