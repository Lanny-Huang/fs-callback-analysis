Engagement-Level View

At the engagement level, 26,682 unique engagements had at least one expert outbound call that the customer missed. Of those, 3,090 engagements (11.6%) had any inbound voice call from the customer at any point — but only 1,288 of the original missed calls (3.6%) had a direct follow-up inbound call after the miss.

Key Takeaways

1. Very low callback rate (~3.6%). When an FS expert calls a customer during an appointment and the customer doesn't answer, fewer than 4 in 100 customers call back. The vast majority of re-engagement happens through expert/system-initiated rescheduling, not customer action.
2. When they do call back, it's immediate. 85% of callbacks happen within 30 minutes of the missed call, suggesting the customer saw a missed call notification and responded right away. Very few call back after more than a few hours.
3. Voice Callback has the highest callback rate (4.5%). When the system-initiated callback goes unanswered, customers are slightly more likely to call back than when an expert places a direct outbound call (3.0%).
4. Rate is stable across the season (3–4%). Despite volume ramping 30x from early January to March, the callback rate barely moves — this is a consistent behavioral pattern, not a seasonal anomaly.
Methodology & Data Source

Source table: ent_care_dwh.rpt_cct_interactions (CCT — Contact Trace Records)

Period: TY25, Jan 2026 – Mar 2026 (leg_start_yearmonth >= 202601)

Business unit: bu = 'cg' (Consumer Group / TurboTax)

Expert missed call definition:
Calls where the expert/system reached out and the customer did not answer:
initiated_type IN ('OUTBOUND VOICE', 'VOICE CALLBACK', 'VOICE CALLBACK SCHEDULED')
AND customer_handle_flg = 0
AND engagement_id IS NOT NULL

Customer callback definition:
Any subsequent inbound voice call on the same engagement:
initiated_type = 'INBOUND VOICE'
AND leg_direction = 'INBOUND'
AND contact_start_ts > missed_call_ts
AND engagement_id = same engagement

Note: An earlier version of this analysis used care_rpt.rpt_appointment_funnel which tracks appointment rebookings (33% rate) — not actual call-level initiation direction. The CCT table is the correct source for identifying who initiated each call.
