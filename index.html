Key Takeaways

1. Experts made 5,928 outbound FS appointment calls TYTD. The customer answer rate is 82.3% — 1 in 6 calls goes unanswered. Volume scales dramatically in peak season (Feb–Mar), reaching 900+ calls/week.
2. 17.5% of missed-call customers attempt a callback — but 71% land in the wrong queue. 168 of 959 customers made an inbound call after missing their FS appointment. However, 120 of them (71%) were routed to DIY Product Support instead of reaching their FS expert. Only 32 reached an FS queue.
3. The routing gap is the opportunity. Customers who call back within the hour are clearly trying to reconnect — median DIY callback is just 54 minutes. A "call your expert back" feature could redirect these 120+ customers to the right place.
4. Customers who call back have better outcomes. 79.8% of callback customers were later handled in an FS call, vs 61.7% of non-callback customers — an 18 pp gap. The callback intent signals higher engagement and urgency.
5. Expert retries within 30 min connect 100% of the time. Of expert retries, those within 30 minutes had a perfect connection rate. Beyond 4 hours, success drops to ~50–60%. Faster retries = better outcomes.
6. ~35% of missed-call customers have no reconnection yet. 337 customers haven't reconnected via any FS channel. Many are recent (Feb–Mar), but this cohort represents potential at-risk engagements.
7. Product completion is too early to measure. Only 4 of 959 affected customers have completed any product. 86% have no PAM record yet — FS returns are still being prepared by experts. This will mature post-April deadline.
Methodology & Data Source

Source table: ent_care_dwh.rpt_cct_interactions (CCT — Contact Trace Records)

Period: TYTD, Nov 2025 – Mar 2026 (leg_start_yearmonth >= 202511)

Scope: bu = 'cg', interaction_skill_function = 'FS', appointment_id IS NOT NULL

Expert outbound call:
All FS appointment calls initiated by the expert/system:
interaction_skill_function = 'FS'
AND appointment_id IS NOT NULL
AND initiated_type NOT IN ('INBOUND VOICE')
Total: 5,928 calls (excluding 681 with null customer_handle_flg from rate calculations)

Customer missed: customer_handle_flg = 0 — 1,050 calls across 959 unique customers (17.7%)

Customer callback: Any initiated_type = 'INBOUND VOICE' call (across all skill functions, not just FS) made by the same auth_id after the missed call. This broad definition captures customers who try to call back but are routed to non-FS queues (e.g., DIY Product Support).

Expert retry: Any subsequent expert-initiated call on same engagement (excluding INBOUND VOICE)

Later FS call handled: Any customer_handle_flg = 1 call with interaction_skill_function = 'FS' for same auth_id after the miss

Product completion: tax_rpt.product_analytics_master (tax_year = 2025), joined on auth_id. 86% of affected customers have no PAM record — FS returns are prepared by experts before the customer reviews and files.
